# Voice Input

Capture your voice and convert it to text using advanced speech recognition. Dashi uses the Groq Whisper model to transcribe your speech in real time.

## Recording Voice

To start recording your voice:

1. Press **Cmd + Option + V** (Mac) or **Ctrl + Alt + V** (Windows/Linux)
2. Hold the keys while speaking
3. Release the keys to stop recording and transcribe

<Note>
Make sure your microphone is enabled in your system settings before using voice input.
</Note>

## Transcription Process

When you release the voice input keys, Dashi automatically:

1. Stops the audio recording
2. Converts the audio to WAV format (16kHz mono PCM)
3. Sends the audio to the Groq Whisper API
4. Transcribes your speech to text
5. Displays the transcription in the chat interface
6. Generates an AI response

The entire process happens seamlessly, requiring no additional steps from you.

## Visual Feedback During Recording

While recording, you'll see visual indicators:

- Animated music notes appear next to Dashi
- Color transitions between teal (#8CEBE5) and magenta (#D500D8)
- The recording state is active and visible

<Steps>
### Step 1: Activate Voice Mode
Press and hold **Cmd + Option + V** to start recording.

### Step 2: Speak Clearly
Speak your command or question while holding the keys.

### Step 3: Release to Transcribe
Release the keys to stop recording and process your speech.

### Step 4: View Results
Your transcribed text appears in the chat, followed by Dashi's response.
</Steps>

## Error Handling

If microphone access is denied, Dashi displays an alert message prompting you to enable microphone permissions in your browser or system settings.

## Supported Audio

Voice input works best with:

- Clear, spoken English
- Minimal background noise
- Normal speaking pace
- Direct microphone input (not external speakers)

<Tip>
For best results, use a quality microphone and speak at a normal pace. The Groq Whisper model handles most accents and speech patterns effectively.
</Tip>
